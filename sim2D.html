Here is a simulation I made of 2D Navier Stokes turbulence in a pipe. The turbulence is driven by boundary conditions which specify that the flow at the boundary of the right end of the pipe is against the direction of flow through the pipe. One can clearly see that small eddies form and merge to form the large stable eddies present at the end of the simulation.
