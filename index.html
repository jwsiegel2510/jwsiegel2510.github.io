I am currently an Assistant Professor at Texas A&M University. Previously I was an Assistant Research Professor in the Mathematics Department at Penn State working with Professor Jinchao Xu.

My research interests include approximation theory, particularly the approximation theory of neural networks, and its application to numerical PDEs. I have also worked on numerical optimization, partial differential equations, deep learning and compressed sensing as well as their applications to materials science. You can find more details on my research here.
