I am currently an Assistant Professor at Texas A&M University. Previously I was an Assistant Research Professor in the Mathematics Department at Penn State working with Professor Jinchao Xu.

My research interests are centered around approximation theory and its adjacent fields of mathematics and science, in particular deep learning, statistics, and numerical methods for solving PDEs. I have also worked on numerical optimization, partial differential equations, deep learning and compressed sensing as well as their applications to materials science. You can find more details on my research here.

I received my PhD from UCLA under the direction of Russel Caflisch. Here is my CV.
